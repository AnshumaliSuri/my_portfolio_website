Instagram Clone Application

Project Details

Imagine a social media app just like Instagram, built with Flutter for a smooth and fast experience. Sign up or log in to connect with friends and family. Share your world through photos and captions, and keep up with others' posts. Browse through feeds, like interesting content, and follow users to see their updates. The app also allows you to unfollow users and log out whenever you want. With familiar features and a user-friendly interface, this app lets you stay connected and share your moments in a snap.
