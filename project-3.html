AI Radio

Project Details

Fueled by AI, this radio app brings a dynamic listening experience to your fingertips. Developed with Flutter for a beautiful and adaptable interface, it seamlessly integrates with the Alan AI API for intuitive voice control. Tune in to your favorite FM stations or explore a diverse range of music categorized for every mood. With voice commands, you can effortlessly navigate genres, switch stations, and personalize your radio experience , all without lifting a finger.
